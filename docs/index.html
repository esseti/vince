Vince

Early is on time. On time is late. Late is unacceptable.

macOS menu bar app that shows your Google Calendar events, counts down to your next meeting, and auto-opens video links so you're never late.

Download Vince {{VERSION}} macOS 12+ • Released {{RELEASE_DATE}} • Free & open source

Menu bar at a glance

Current event + live countdown shown directly in your menu bar. No extra windows.

Auto-open meetings

One minute before start, Vince opens the Meet/Zoom/Teams link automatically.

Meeting timer popup

Color-coded timer (green → yellow → red) shows how much time is left in the current meeting.

Slack DND sync

Sets your Slack status to Do Not Disturb while you're in a meeting.
