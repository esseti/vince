"Early is on time. On time is late.."
Vince Lombardi

Vince is a tiny macOS app that shows your next Google Calendar meeting in the menu bar, counts down to the start, and opens the call link automatically — so you're never late.

Download for macOS View on GitHub →
Free & open source · macOS 12 or later · Universal binary
Features

Small app. Big focus on time.

Four jobs, done quietly in the background, every day.

◷

Live countdown in the menu bar

The current event and the time remaining sit right next to your clock. One glance — you know where you stand.

↗

Auto-opens your meeting links

One minute before the meeting starts, Vince opens the Meet, Zoom or Teams link from the event description. You walk in early, every time.

⏱

Floating timer popup

An optional popup shows how much time is left in the current meeting, with a color that shifts as the clock runs down.

●

Slack Do Not Disturb

While you're in a meeting, Vince flips Slack to DND and sets your status. When the meeting ends, it puts everything back.

How it works

Up and running in two minutes.

Download, sign in to Google, optionally connect Slack — that's it.

1

Download & install

Unzip and drag Vince into Applications. macOS 12 or newer.

2

Connect Google Calendar

Sign in once. Vince only needs read access to your events.

3

Optional: connect Slack

Add a token and Vince will manage your DND and status during meetings.
